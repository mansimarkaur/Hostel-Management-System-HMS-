Info & Policies

Students need to bring their own mattresses while we provide beds. We have several communal areas, like a living room with TV, a badminton court and attached balconies for each floor.

Breakfast, Lunch, Snacks and Dinner is serverd in the mess daily. Menu is decided by the students themselves.
